The BreakFast Company Journey

Established since 1958. Quenching the hunger of that 1st meal of the day.
Breakfast is considered as the most important meal and it should be healthy and rich.
Our motive is to serve our beloved customers the food and the protine that one requires a day to start with.

About the Idea

The founder Mr. Gurprit Singh was fighting the world war for the nation in the year 1940, Far away from home. He realised the need for a rich and healthy breakfast to fight the war. After the war in the year 1950 he realised that half of the nation is not getting the adequate protine for the day .So on Oct 14, 1950 he decided to start the misson of feeding the people and we are just carrying his dream till today.
